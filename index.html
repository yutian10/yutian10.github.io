Short Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at MMLab, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Tianfan Xue. Before that, I received my Bachelor's degree in Robotics Engineering from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Yue Wang. I also spent a wonderful time at Shanghai AI Laboratory as a research intern, mentored by Dr. Shi Guo.
